About Music Sheet Instructions

Instructions

Following Tools Required:

-Firefox(recommended) or Chrome

-Microphone

-Musical Instrument(Optional, but highly recommended)

1. Enter the home menu where the music sheet resides by clicking the Home on the top left to begin the application

2. To get started, click on one of the four buttons labeled whole note, half note, quarter note, and eighth you wish to insert into the music sheet

3. Click on the button that says "Use Mic" to begin recording. If this is your first time clicking on the button, a window with prompt up underneath the address bar asking for permission. Click Yes to activate your microphone.

Now you may begin recording your sheet music by playing your audio through the Microphone.

Additional Information:

-You can change note beats during recording

-A note with a green background color represents a sharp key

-You can turn off your Microphone located on the Address Bar

-A music pitch detector is located above the buttons that will tell if you are in tune

-By pressing the Clear button, you will refresh the page and any progress you have
